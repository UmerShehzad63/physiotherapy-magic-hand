Back Pain & Lower Back Pain

Back pain is the most common musculoskeletal complaint, affecting over 80% of people. It can be acute or chronic with many possible causes.

Common symptoms:

  • Constant or intermittent pain in the lower back
  • Stiffness, restricted movement
  • Radiating pain into the leg
  • Pain when bending or lifting

Our treatment: Manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, soft tissue therapy. Manual Therapy ‚Üí

Neck Pain & Stiffness

Neck pain is especially common among office workers. Caused by poor posture, stress, or cervical spine problems.

Common symptoms:

  • Neck stiffness, limited head rotation
  • Radiating pain into shoulder or arm
  • Headache from the base of the neck
  • Numbness in the arms

Our treatment: Manual therapy, cervical mobilisation, ergonomic consulting. Details ‚Üí

Shoulder Pain & Frozen Shoulder

Frozen shoulder is inflammation and thickening of the joint capsule, causing progressive loss of range of motion.

Common symptoms:

  • Progressively worsening range of motion
  • Night pain
  • Difficulty raising the arm
  • Difficulty getting dressed

Our treatment: Manual therapy, mobilisation, targeted therapeutic exercise. Details ‚Üí

Herniated Disc

A herniated disc occurs when the inner material of intervertebral discs pushes out and puts pressure on nerve roots.

Common symptoms:

  • Intense, sharp spinal pain
  • Radiating pain into leg or arm
  • Numbness, tingling
  • Muscle weakness

Our treatment: Spinal therapy, McKenzie method, core stabilisation. Spinal Therapy ‚Üí

Sciatica

Pain along the sciatic nerve, radiating from the lower back down the back of the leg. Most commonly caused by a herniated disc.

Common symptoms:

  • Sharp, burning pain from buttock to foot
  • Numbness or tingling in the leg
  • Muscle weakness in the leg
  • Pain when sitting

Our treatment: Manual therapy, nerve mobilisation, targeted exercises. Details ‚Üí

Headache & Migraine

Many headaches originate from the cervical spine or muscle tension and can be effectively treated with physiotherapy.

Common symptoms:

  • Recurring headache from the back of the head
  • Tension headache from sedentary work
  • Migraine-like symptoms with cervical origin

Our treatment: Cervical manual therapy, trigger point therapy, postural correction.

Knee Problems

The knee is one of the most commonly injured joints, especially among athletes. Effectively treated conservatively.

Common conditions:

  • Runner's knee (patellofemoral syndrome)
  • IT band syndrome
  • Meniscus injuries
  • ACL/PCL injury rehabilitation

Our treatment: Sports physiotherapy, rehabilitation, muscle strengthening. Sports Physiotherapy ‚Üí

Tennis Elbow / Golfer's Elbow

Pain on the outer or inner side of the elbow caused by overuse of forearm muscles and tendons.

Common symptoms:

  • Pain when gripping or lifting
  • Weakness in the hand
  • Tenderness around the elbow

Our treatment: Soft tissue therapy, eccentric exercises, Kinesio Tape.

Plantar Fasciitis

Inflammation of the fascia on the sole of the foot, causing sharp heel pain especially with the first steps in the morning.

  • Sharp heel pain in the morning
  • Pain after prolonged standing
  • Tenderness in the arch of the foot

Our treatment: Manual therapy, stretching, shockwave therapy, insole advice.

Ankle Problems

Ankle sprains and chronic ankle instability can be effectively improved with physiotherapy.

  • Post-ankle sprain rehabilitation
  • Chronic instability
  • Achilles tendon problems

Our treatment: Mobilisation, proprioceptive training, taping. Sports Physiotherapy ‚Üí

Scoliosis

Lateral curvature of the spine causing postural problems and pain. Treatable with targeted exercises.

  • Asymmetric shoulder or hip height
  • Back pain under load
  • Postural imbalance

Our treatment: Schroth method, core stabilisation, postural correction. Spinal Therapy ‚Üí

TMJ (Jaw Joint) Problems

Temporomandibular joint dysfunction can cause pain, clicking, and limited movement during chewing.

  • Pain when chewing
  • Clicking sound in the jaw
  • Difficulty opening the mouth
  • Pain around the ear

Our treatment: Manual therapy, soft tissue techniques, relaxation exercises.

Carpal Tunnel Syndrome

A nerve compression syndrome of the wrist causing numbness, pain, and weakness in the hand.

  • Numbness in fingers (thumb, index, middle)
  • Night pain and tingling
  • Grip strength weakness

Our treatment: Nerve mobilisation, wrist mobilisation, ergonomic consulting.

Pregnancy-Related Pain

Hormonal and postural changes during pregnancy often cause lower back, pelvic, and back pain.

  • Lower back pain during pregnancy
  • Pelvic floor pain (SPD)
  • Sciatica during pregnancy

Our treatment: Safe manual therapy, pregnancy exercise therapy, pelvic floor strengthening.

Post-Surgical Conditions

Post-surgical rehabilitation is essential for full functional recovery and prevention of complications.

  • Knee and hip replacement rehabilitation
  • Shoulder fracture rehabilitation
  • ACL reconstruction rehabilitation
  • Post-spinal surgery rehabilitation

Our treatment: Comprehensive rehabilitation program. Rehabilitation ‚Üí
